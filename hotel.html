The Situation

The Process

Simple for your team.
Extraordinary for your guest.

No new software. No additional staff. We integrate into your existing booking workflow.

Share your upcoming arrivals

Send us your guest list — names, arrival dates, room types, any preferences from the booking notes. We handle everything with full confidentiality. A secure transfer takes under 5 minutes.

We craft each personalized video

Your hotel's visual identity, the guest's name on screen, a preview of their suite, your GM's welcome, and your chosen upsells — all woven into a single 60–90 second video per guest. Delivered within 48 hours.

Guests receive a private link

Each guest gets their unique link via WhatsApp, email, or SMS — 3–5 days before arrival. When they open it and see their name on screen, they feel something. That feeling is what they remember when they check in.
