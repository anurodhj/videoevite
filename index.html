How VideoEvite Works

A simple, automated workflow that processes guest data lists, layers personalized overlays, and emails or texts custom videos to individual recipients.

01

Record & Upload

Upload a high-quality video of the host, GM, or Dean inviting or welcoming guests.

02

Map Variables

Select which parts of the video card dynamically fetch guest names, dates, or titles from your data.

03

Sync Customer Data

Upload a CSV file or link directly via booking systems, CRMs, or our Developer API.

04

Auto-Personalize

Our cloud engine automatically generates personalized video pages, sent via Email, SMS, or WhatsApp.
